Client Review

Nandita Sinha is the Chief Executive Officer at Myntra, one of India’s leading fashion and beauty platforms. In her career spanning two decades, Nandita has contributed to shaping various facets of the Indian retail and e-commerce industry while driving growth and innovation. She has been on a mission to redefine the boundaries of how digital retail can enhance the lives of consumers and the larger ecosystem at scale. A champion of innovation, she strongly affirms its role in creating opportunities for organizational growth and driving industry disruption. Before joining Myntra, Nandita helmed various verticals during her stint at Flipkart, helping brands and sellers to effectively cater to the evolving needs of Indian shoppers. As the CEO of Myntra, a pioneering force in the online shopping space in India, Nandita is leading the organisation towards its vision of democratizing fashion and beauty using technology. Nandita has groomed and mentored several future leaders helping them scale their true potential, in addition to being an active sponsor of inclusion and empowerment. In 2023, she was featured by the Economic Times in its ‘Women Ahead’ list, and is a part of Forbes 2024 W-Power List.

NANDITA SINHA

FIRST WOMEN CEO OF MYNTRA
